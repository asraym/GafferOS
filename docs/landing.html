What GafferOS Does

Six Engines.
One Report.

01
⬡
Formation Engine

Evaluates your attacking strength, defensive vulnerability, and opponent threat to select the optimal shape — from a 4-3-3 to a 5-4-1 — before a ball is kicked.

02
⚡
Press Intensity

Squad fatigue is never ignored. The press engine recommends High, Medium, or Low intensity based on your players' fitness — so you never burn out your side.

03
◈
Squad Selection

Builds your starting XI and bench from available players, weighted by position-specific impact scores and fitness — your best eleven, calculated.

04
◎
Match Risk

A composite risk score — factoring opponent strength, defensive fragility, fatigue, and current form — tells you exactly how much danger you're walking into.

05
⟁
Mismatch Detection

Surfaces statistical advantages to exploit and threats to neutralise. No more guessing where you're stronger — the data tells you plainly.

06
✦
Tactical Reasoning

Every recommendation comes with a plain-English paragraph explaining the why — so you can brief your players with confidence, not just a spreadsheet.
